Pancit

Description

The national dish of the Philippines. It is a healthy meal for the whole family, or when you got friends visiting.

Ingredients

  • 1pkg of Pancit noodles
  • 400g of chicken
  • 4 Carrots
  • Some Soup Veg
  • Holy Trinity of Soy Sauce (light, dark and calamansi flavoured)
  • 200g of Shrimps

Steps

  1. Chop all the veg roghly. Add them to a large pot and saute them with little oil.
  2. Add all 3 Soy Sauces and some water. Let it simmer for 20 minutes.
  3. Add the chicken and cook for 1 hour, until tender. Add the shrimps in the last 10 Minutes.
  4. Finally add the Pancit Noodles and let them soak all the goodness of the sauce.
  5. Serve with a bowl of rice and a Calamansi.
