Biryani

Description

This is one of my most favourite Pakistani dish! Hyderabad Biryani has rich flavor, fluffy rice and delicous chicken.

Ingredients

  • 1kg of rice
  • 400g of chicken
  • 4 Paprika
  • 4 Green Chillies
  • 4 Onions
  • 1 Whole Garlic
  • some spices

Steps

  1. Wash the rice throughly, at least 2-3 times, until water runs clear. Let it soke in water for at least 30 minutes
  2. Chop onions and garlic finely, and saute them in a large pot. Preferably use Ghee.
  3. Add the chicken and the spices. Let them roast until fragant
  4. Finally add the Chillies and Paprika until they soften and get fragant.
  5. Add desired amount of water. Take care you dont add too much, since the rice has to soak it all up.
  6. After letting it simmer for about 1 hour, add the rice and let it cook until all the water is gone.
  7. Serve the Biryani with fresh Chapati and Youghurt.
