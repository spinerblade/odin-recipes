Pasta Aglio Olio

Description

This is a very simple and quick dish. Perfect for your midnight snack or after work meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pkg of Pasta
  • Olive Oil
  • 4 Red Chillies
  • 1 Whole Garlic

Steps

  1. Chop onions and garlic finely, and saute them in a large pot. Use generous amount of Olive Oil
  2. Boil the Pasta. Stop 2 Minutes early, since they will cook finished in the pan with our Aglio Olio
  3. Add the Red Chillies to the pan and the boiled Pasta
  4. Stir them nicely. You can add some Pasta water, if you want more creaminess
  5. Serve with some Parmesan.
